Crop Diseases & Remedies

Powdery Mildew

Prevent with proper spacing and airflow. Treat with neem oil or sulfur-based products.

Blight

Rotate crops to avoid reinfection. Use copper-based fungicides as a treatment.

Root Rot

Improve soil drainage and reduce overwatering to prevent root rot.
